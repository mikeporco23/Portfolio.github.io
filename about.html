About Me

After graduating from SUNY Purchase with a degree in Communications, I broadened my horizons by moving to Japan, where I lived for three years. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I began learning Python Programming, and automation. Since then, I have been avidly using these tools in various aspects of my career. Including teaching English in Japan, managing restaurants in New York, and brokering Health Insurance.

I have a passion for storytelling and believe in making data-driven conclusions clear and compelling. The endless possibilities of data analytics excite me, as I see it as a tool for creating meaningful insights and driving decisions.

Previously a licensed life and health insurance agent, I am transitioning away from sales to make a more significant impact through data. I am dedicated to lifelong learning and continuous improvement.
