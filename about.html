About Me

After graduating from SUNY Purchase with a degree in Communications, I broadened my horizons by moving to Japan, where I was a resident's assistant at a college dorm, an English Assistant Language Teacher in a Middle and Elementary School, and Head Tutor at my own English Language learning center through Sasebo Nova. During the COVID-19 pandemic, I began learning Python Programming, and automation. Since then, I have been avidly using these tools in various aspects of my career. Including teaching English in Japan, managing restaurants in New York, and brokering Health Insurance.

Aspiring leasing professional with 5+ years of client-facing experience in sales, hospitality, and education. Skilled in managing high-volume interactions, resolving customer concerns, and streamlining operations. Eager to bring strong communication and compliance expertise to a dynamic Seattle property management team.

Previously a licensed life and health insurance agent, I am transitioning away from sales to make a more significant impact in fostering community. I am dedicated to lifelong learning, and passionate about engaging with others.
